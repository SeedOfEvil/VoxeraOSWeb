What Voxera OS does

AI should ask, not assume.

Three jobs. One coherent system. Every action stays inside the boundary you set.

📥

Turn intent into jobs

Natural language goals become structured jobs. Each one is processed predictably, one at a time, with a full record of what happened.

🔒

Gate risk with approvals

Actions that carry risk require your explicit sign-off. Policy decides what needs approval at runtime. Nothing bypasses the gate.

📋

Prove what happened

Every job produces a structured record: the plan, the actions taken, and the outcome. Complete traceability, every time.
