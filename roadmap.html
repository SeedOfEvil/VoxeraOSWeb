Roadmap · After v0.1.5

What comes after the Alpha baseline

Alpha v0.1.5 locks in operator-grade artifact hygiene on top of a solid daily-driver foundation. The next milestones focus on reliability signals, safer planning previews, and stronger provider behavior before broader voice expansion.

Near-term

Panel auth hardening & graceful daemon shutdown

Rate limiting on panel auth endpoints and clean SIGTERM handling in the queue daemon for production-grade service reliability.

Near-term

Brain fallback error classification in voxera doctor

Surface brain fallback reason enums (TIMEOUT, AUTH, RATE_LIMIT, MALFORMED, NETWORK) directly in the doctor output for faster diagnosis.

Next 4 weeks

Structured mission planning previews

Richer dry-run simulation UX with explicit operator confirmation pathways and clearer capability usage summaries before committing to a plan.

Next 8 weeks

Provider behavior hardening & mission catalog expansion

Stronger OpenAI-compatible and Ollama provider behavior, improved fallback chain handling, and a broader mission catalog with 10+ built-in workflows.

v0.3

Voice stack

STT/TTS integration, wake word detection, and a voice-first command loop. The "Hey Voxera" experience becomes real.

v0.4

Signed skills, marketplace & image packaging

Signed skill bundles, a community skill marketplace, and ISO/image packaging for clean machine provisioning.

Alpha positioning

Trustworthy baseline before broader expansion

Alpha v0.1.5 is intentionally focused: stable queue operations, operator-grade hygiene, and strong safety gates — built to be relied on daily, not just demoed.
